Interseting Classes

Strength


Strength class is a dynamic and empowering fitness program designed to enhance muscular strength, endurance, and overall physical fitness. It combines elements of weightlifting and resistance training to challenge and strengthen the body.



Yoga


Yoga class offers a tranquil and rejuvenating experience that combines physical movements, breath control, and meditation techniques to promote harmony and well-being. It is a centuries-old practice that originated in ancient India and has gained widespread popularity worldwide.


HIIT&Athletic


HIIT & Athletic class is an energizing and intense fitness program that combines the principles of High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) with athletic-inspired movements and exercises. It is designed to maximize cardiovascular endurance, build strength, and improve overall athletic performance.


cost: Free if you have membership!

Training Programs

Personal Training


Personal training class offers a personalized and focused fitness experience tailored to meet individual goals and needs. It involves working closely with a certified personal trainer who provides one-on-one guidance, support, and accountability throughout the fitness journey.


cost: 45/h


Group Training


Group fitness training class provides a motivating and inclusive environment where individuals come together to achieve their fitness goals in a supportive group setting. Led by a skilled instructor, these classes offer a wide range of workout options that cater to different fitness levels and interests.


cost: 20/h
